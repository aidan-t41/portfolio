Sol Protector

I made this game for a programming class as the final project. It's pretty basic, but as my first "full" game, I learned a lot making it and I think it turned out okay. It's a simple 2D space-shooter, where you control a ship and shoot oncoming meteors and UFOs to destroy them, earning money which can be spent at the shop to upgrade your ship and weapons.


Overview
  • Simple, fast-paced space combat
  • Powerups
  • Various enemies & meteors to destroy
  • Money & shop system
